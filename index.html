How to Use

  • First, select your Floor, Room, and Cup from the sidebar.
  • Then, enter your name and MID to start voting.
  • Click on the "Admin Mode" button (if you have admin access) to add or remove menu items.
  • As a regular user, you can vote for your favorite menu items.
  • Each user can vote only once per menu item.
  • Each user may vote for up to 2 items per category. After you cast 2 votes in a category, that category is closed for you and you cannot vote more items in it.
  • Use the heart icon to show your preference for a dish.
  • Use the "Filter by Dish Category" dropdown to view specific types of dishes.
