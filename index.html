How to Use

  • First, select your Floor and Room from the sidebar, or select a Manager.
  • If you are a room, click "Start Voting" to begin.
  • If you are a manager, select your name, then select the Floor and Room you are supervising, and click "Start Voting" to vote on behalf of that room.
  • Click on the "Admin Mode" button (if you have admin access) to add or remove menu items.
  • As a regular user (room or manager supervising a room), you can vote for your favorite menu items.
  • Each room can vote only once per menu item.
  • Each room may vote for up to 2 items per category. After you cast 2 votes in a category, that category is closed for you and you cannot vote more items in it.
  • Use the heart icon to show your preference for a dish.
  • Use the "Filter by Dish Category" dropdown to view specific types of dishes.
  • Managers can view the voting status but cannot vote directly for themselves; they vote on behalf of a room.
